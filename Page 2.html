Holiday Activities

There are many destinations in Wales full of activites for holidays. Some examples are:

Caerphilly - This town next to North Cardiff offers guided tours, has a castle in the middle of the town and has many shops to visit. There is a guide centre not far fromt eh castle with daily guides. There are bakeries and has a park nearby for children to play in. Caerphilly town is mainly on a hill but offers a lot in shopping with many take out restaurants and clothes stores within, not too far away is a small industrial estate with many fast food chains there for people who want something to eat.

Cwmbran - There is an indoor mall with lot of shops and has regular festivals in the main sqaure of the shopping district, there are many festivals held around the area also. There are many cafes and there are a lot parks for children to play in.

These are some of the more popular places in Wales to go on holiday, they show Welsh culture and give a good experience of the country.
